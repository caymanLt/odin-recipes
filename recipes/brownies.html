Simple Homemade Brownies

Description

Simple brownies that can be made with only 6 ingredients you probably already have.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175C
  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan, let cool
  3. Whisk in the cocoa, sugar and vanilla
  4. Stir in the eggs, one at a time then add the flour
  5. Bake in the oven, 20-25 minutes
  6. Let cool for a minimum of 45 minutes
